Our Free Transport Delivery Radii

From our central headquarters, we safely dispatch shipments across three core zones without charging a single mileage fee.

Zone A (Immediate Transit)

Deliveries within a 50-mile radius are processed and completed within 24 to 48 hours of health check clearings.

Zone B (Regional Freight)

Deliveries ranging from 51 to 200 miles drop every Tuesday and Thursday using mid-tier biosecure climate-vans.

Zone C (Extended Haul)

Long-distance transfers (200+ miles) go out in weekly Friday convoys to maximize animal company and minimize stress.
